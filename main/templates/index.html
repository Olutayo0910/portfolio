Olutayo Victor Ogunlade is a seasoned full stack software engineer and the visionary founder and CEO of Tay & Becca Services. Starting his career as a sales executive and advancing to sales director, Olutayo developed innovative sales strategies and co-authored "What Your Customer Wants to Hear." His expertise extends to technology, where he has created various software products like Flexiwork, Casjobs, and AI Blog Generator, all aimed at enhancing business efficiency and productivity.

Driven by a passion for technology, Olutayo self-taught himself software engineering and earned a certificate in full stack software engineering from ALX Africa. This unique blend of skills allows him to leverage technology to streamline business operations and boost sales. Olutayo is a firm believer in the transformative power of artificial intelligence, viewing it as a powerful tool for productivity rather than a competitor. His forward-thinking perspective on AI and its role in the future of work is a recurring theme in the many blog articles he has written.

What sets Olutayo apart is his holistic approach to technology, stemming from his rich background in sales and business management. He trains individuals in business strategies, personal development, and tech skills, making him a sought-after mentor. Balancing his professional endeavors with personal commitments, Olutayo is married and a proud father. His journey from sales to technology, and his innovative use of AI, positions him as a unique leader in the tech industry, driving both business success and technological advancement through Tay & Becca Services.
